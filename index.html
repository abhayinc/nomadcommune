// MANIFESTO

"Leave the resorts to the tourists. The wild is where we build."

We've traded boring hotel rooms and generic co-working desks for something real. Workations shouldn't mean sitting inside a glass box ordering room service.

We build workspaces in Kumaoni mud-houses, run expeditions into desert camps under starry Thar skies, and share meals cooked over log wood fires. We build for the engineer, the designer, and the writer who need solid power backups and fiber internet just as much as they need silence, dirt roads, and real local culture.
